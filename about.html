Our story

Here at Foster & Sims we have been looking after customers in Lincolnshire since 1958. We specialise in giving outstanding service by making sure we have all the most up-to-date vehicle manufacturers' equipment, software and highly trained staff.

We're members of HEVRA (the Hybrid and Electric Vehicle Repair Alliance), so you can be confident we have the correct tools, training and safety procedures to look after EVs and hybrids too.
